You need Python 3.9.0 and pygame to properly run this game.

Press 'P' or 'p' to play. Or click "Play" with your mouse.

You walk forward with the up arrow and backwards with the
down arrow. You turn left and right with the left and right
arrows, respectively.

Press 'q' or 'Q' to quit, or quit python.
